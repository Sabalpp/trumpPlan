## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Political Sentiment Alpha Platform ("we," "our," "Platform") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our service.

**Key Principles**:
- We collect **minimal personal data**
- We do **NOT** track individual trading behavior
- We do **NOT** sell data to third parties
- We comply with applicable privacy laws (GLBA, GDPR principles)

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- **Waitlist/Account Creation**: Email address only
- **Payment Information**: Processed by Stripe; we never store credit card details
- **Referral Code**: Optional, for waitlist rewards

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data**: API endpoint requests, timestamps (for analytics only)
- **Technical Data**: IP address (temporary, for security), browser type, device type
- **Cookies**: Minimal session cookies for functionality

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

- ❌ Trading account credentials
- ❌ Portfolio holdings or balances
- ❌ Individual trade execution data
- ❌ Social Security Numbers or Tax IDs
- ❌ Browsing history outside our platform
- ❌ Location tracking (beyond country-level for compliance)

### 4.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We share minimal data with:
- **Stripe**: Payment processing (PCI-DSS compliant)
- **AWS**: Cloud hosting (encrypted storage)
- **Monitoring Services**: Aggregated performance metrics

**We do NOT**:
- Sell data to advertisers
- Share data with brokers or financial institutions
- Disclose individual user activity to third parties

### 4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose data if required by:
- Valid court order or subpoena
- Legal obligation (e.g., SEC investigation)
- Protection of rights/safety (e.g., fraud prevention)
